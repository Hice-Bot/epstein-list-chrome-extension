## Overview

Epstein List Highlighter is a Chrome extension that highlights names of people associated with the Epstein files on webpages you visit.

## Data Collection

**This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data whatsoever.**

- No personal information is collected
- No browsing history is tracked
- No data is sent to any server
- No analytics or telemetry is used
- No cookies are set

## How It Works

The extension runs entirely in your browser. It scans the text content of webpages locally on your device to identify matching names and applies visual highlights. All processing happens client-side with zero network activity from the extension.

## Permissions

The extension requires access to all URLs solely to scan page text for listed names. No data from any page is collected, stored, or transmitted.
